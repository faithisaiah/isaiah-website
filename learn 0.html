POSITION

Position
Distance of an Object from a Reference Point.
Point A to Point B or Position A to Position B.

Position is a Vector Quantity

DISPLACEMENT

Displacement
Change of body or of an Object position.
Measured in metres.
Change from position A to position B in a Rectilinear or
curvilinear Motion.

Displacement is a Vector Quantity
Displacement = Final position - Intial position
Final position subtracted by initial position

Rigid Body

Motion of Rigid Body
Displacement of Rigid Body is called
Linear Displacement
Rotation of Rigid Body is called
Angular Displacement.

Displacement is a Vector Quantity
